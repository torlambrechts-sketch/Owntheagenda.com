How it works

The workshop is the hero. The loop is the proof.

Pick a team challenge and OwnTheAgenda walks you through a facilitator-grade session — start to follow-through — without anyone facilitating but you.

1

Assess

A short, validated pulse captures how your team really works — trust, alignment, role clarity, decisions, communication. Minutes per person.

2

Generate

We turn this team's results into an opinionated, time-boxed agenda — a run-of-show with prompts, not a blank canvas.

3

Run

Step through it live: sectioned agenda, a timer, per-step prompts, and lightweight capture. You lead the room; we keep the time.

4

Commit

Decisions and owners are captured in the room and surfaced afterward as tracked actions nobody can quietly drop.

5

Re-measure

Re-run the pulse next cadence and watch the movement. The trend is what makes the work stick.

It compounds every cadence carries actions forward and builds a longitudinal picture of team health.
Why OwnTheAgenda

Built for the leader in the room — not the dashboard you forget.

Plenty of tools profile your people. OwnTheAgenda puts that intelligence to work in the session itself, then proves the session moved something.

You run it — yourself

A run-of-show any leader can follow, with the prompts and timing a great facilitator would bring. No certification, no third party to schedule.

Grounded in real science

An owned psychometric engine for how each leader is wired, fused with a team-dynamics layer for how you work together. Every agenda is built from your data — never a generic template.

The session is the product

A structured live surface — agenda, timer, prompts, capture — not an infinite whiteboard and not a report you read alone. Everything points at the conversation in the room.

A loop, not a one-off

Recurring cadence, carried-over actions, and a trend line you can show the team and the board. Progress you can actually see.

The assessment core

It knows this team — not a template.

A short pulse maps your team against a healthy range across the dynamics that decide whether a leadership team performs. The result fuels the agenda automatically.

  • Individual psychometrics — how each leader is wired
  • Team dynamics — trust, safety, conflict, role clarity, decision rights
  • Privacy-first and consent-based — bands and ranges, never a score to beat
